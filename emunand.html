Creating an EmuNAND

To complete this guide, Luma3DS needs to be installed.

Please do not follow this guide, if you don't know what exactly you are doing. For the average user, an EmuNAND is completely useless, and it will only create issues or damage your SD. If you don't have a good reason to create one, don't follow this.

During this guide, your SD Card will get formatted. It will go through backing everything up, but if you don't follow that, you will lose everything on your SD Card.

If you have any issues during the process of this guide, you can get help on the Nintendo Homebrew Discord

What you need:

  • the latest release of GodMode9
  • an SD Card bigger than 2GB
  • the latest release of FBI

Prep Work

  1. Insert your SD Card into your computer
  2. Copy everything from the SD Card to a safe location on your computer
    • If you skip this step, everything on your SD Card will be lost, and can not be recovered
  3. If you haven't already, put GodMode9.firm from the GodMode9 release to /luma/payloads on your SD Card
  4. Insert the SD Card back in your device

Section I - Formatting the SD Card

  1. Hold Start before pressing the power button, to get into GodMode9
  2. Press the HOME button, to open the HOME Menu in GodMode9
    • This is not the regular 3DS Home menu
  3. Select More... and choose SD format Menu
  4. As the size, select SysNAND (full), press A to continue
  5. As the cluster size, select Auto
  6. Optional: Select a Label for your SD Card
  7. Press A to continue, and input the key combo given to format the SD Card
  8. Wait until the process finished, and press Start and R at the same time to power off your device
  9. Insert the SD Card back in your computer, and copy all the files back to your SD Card

Section II - Unlinking the EmuNAND

  1. Insert the SD Card back in your device
  2. Boot your console while holding the L button, to launch your EmuNAND
    • If you get a Failed to mount CTRNAND error, reboot your device while holding L
    • To confirm that you are running your EmuNAND, you can open the system settings, if your firmware version starts with Emu, the installation was successful
    • If some of your installed DSiWare titles only have a blank icon and a blank name, you can use Cthulhu to refresh your icon cache
  3. Open up your System Settings, and go to "Other Settings"
  4. Go to the last page, and choose "Format System Memory"
    • An Internet Connection is required. If you need to do it offline, you can use TinyFormat
  5. Follow the instructions to format your EmuNAND
  6. You now need to set up the basic information like your language, birthday, date etc. again

Section III - Injecting FBI

  1. Power off your device, and reboot holding start to launch GodMode9
  2. Navigate to [0]: SDCARD and navigate to your FBI.cia
  3. Press A on it, then select CIA image options... and then Mount image to drive
  4. Press A on the .app file, and select NCCH image options then Inject to H&S (EmuNAND)
  5. Wait until the process finished, and shut down your device by pressing Start and R
  6. Reboot by holding L to get into your EmuNAND
  7. Open up Health & Safety to launch FBI
  8. In FBI, navigate to your FBI.cia and install it
  9. Reboot your Console while holding Start, to get into GodMode9
  10. Press HOME to open the GodMode9 Home menu, and select More... and then
    Restore H&S (EmuNAND)
  11. Press A to continue and input the key combo given

Done! You can now hold L on boot to get into your EmuNAND, or boot holding Select and choose Autoboot EmuNAND, if you want to automatically load your EmuNAND
