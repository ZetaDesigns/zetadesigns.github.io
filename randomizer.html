How to randomize 3DS Pokémon games (CIA)

This guide will show you how to randomize a CIA pokemon game.

This is a permanent change! This guide will create a second cia, which is completely randomized.

What you will need:

    On your PC:

    • the latest release of pk3DS
    • the latest release of HackingToolKit3DS

    On your 3DS:

    • the latest release of GodMode9

Section I - Creating the CIA

Physical cartridge:

  1. Boot into GodMode9 by holding down Start before pressing the power button
  2. Navigate to [C:] GAMECART
  3. On your gamecart, you will find a file called titleID.trim.3ds
  4. Select this file, then select NCSD image options... and then Build CIA from file
  5. Shut down your console, take out your SD card and insert it into your PC

Digital Game:

  1. Boot into GodMode9 by holding down Start before pressing the power button
  2. Navigate to [A:] SYSNAND SD
  3. Navigate to title
  4. Navigate to the first 8 digits of your TitleID
    • If you don't know what your TitleID is, you can look it up here
  5. Navigate to the second 8 digits of your TitleID
  6. Navigate to content
  7. Press A on the .tmd file
  8. Select TMD file options and then Build CIA (standard)
  9. Shut down your console, take out your SD card and insert it into your PC

Section II - Extracting and randomizing the game

  1. Extract the contents of the PackEnglishVX.zip
  2. Move the CIA you just created to the PackHack folder from the HackingToolKit
  3. Launch SetupUS, and install the ToolKit
  4. Launch HackingToolKit3DS
  5. Type CE to extract the contents of your CIA
    • This process can take some time
  6. Move the ExtractedRomFS and ExtractedExeFS to a seperate folder
  7. Launch pk3DS, and open the folder you put ExtractedRomFS and ExtractedExeFS into
  8. Randomize your game, then save
  9. Put the two folders back into /Packhack/, where the other game files are

Section III - Rebuilding and installing the CIA

  1. Launch HackingToolKit3ds again
  2. Enter CR to rebuild the CIA
  3. When prompted, enter the name for your modded CIA, this will take pretty long
  4. Enter “0” two times
  5. After the CIA has been created, copy it onto your SD, then reinsert it into your 3ds
  6. Launch FBI
  7. Install your modified CIA

Done! You can now launch your game, and it should be randomized!
