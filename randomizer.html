How to randomize 3DS Pokemon games (CIA method)

What you need

on your PC:

• the latest release of pk3DS
• the latest release of HackingToolKit3ds

on your 3ds:

• The latest release of GodMode9

Section I - Creating the CIA

Physical cartridge

1. Boot GodMode9 by holding down [Start] before pressing the power button
2. Navigate to [C:] GAMECART
3. On your gamecart, you will find titleID.trim.3ds
4. Select this file, then select “NCSD image options... → Build CIA from file”
5. Shut off your 3ds, take out your SD card and insert it into your PC

Digital game

1. Boot into GodMode9 by holding [Start] before pressing the power button
2. Navigate to [A:] SYSNAND SD
3. Navigate to “title”
4. Navigate to the first 8 digits of your TitleID
5. Navigate to the second 8 digits of your TitleID
6. Navigate to “content”
7. Press A on the .tmd file
8. Select TMD file options... → Build CIA (standard)

Section II - Extracting and randomizing the game

1. Put the CIA you just created in /Packhack/ from the PackEnglishVX.zip
2. Launch HackingToolKit3ds
3. Enter CE to extract the contents of your CIA (this will take some time)
4. You will end up with the two folders “ExtractedRomFS” and “ExtractedExeFS”
5. Put those two folders into a seperate folder
6. Open pk3ds, then select the folder you just created (with ExtractedRomFS and ExtractedExeFS inside)
7. Randomize your game, then save
8. Put the two folders back into /Packhack/, where the other game files are

Section III - Rebuilding and installing CIA

1. Launch HackingToolKit3ds again
2. Enter CR to rebuild the CIA
3. When prompted, enter the name for your modded CIA, this will take pretty long
4. Enter “0” two times
5. After the CIA has been created, copy it onto your SD, then reinsert it into your 3ds
6. Launch FBI
7. Navigate to the CIA on your SD card and install it

Done!
